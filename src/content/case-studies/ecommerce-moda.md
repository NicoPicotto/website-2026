## The problem

The online store had a cart abandonment rate of 78%. The experience was confusing and the visual identity didn't reflect the brand's proposition.

## Solution

End-to-end redesign: information architecture, design system, and a new look & feel aligned with the brand.

## Impact

Cart abandonment reduced to 52% and average order value increased by 23%.
